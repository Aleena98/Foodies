SOUTH AFRICA

Whilst not so popular like other cuisines out there - South African food is a great treat, especially for all the meat lovers.

Click on the below icons to explore more about South African cuisine.

MAIN DISHES

Boerewors

Definitely for the meat lovers. Made of the best local beef and cooked up on the smoking barbecue.

Bunny chow

Despite the name, no bunnies were hurt during making of this beautiful curry. Usually made with chicken, lamb or mince and served by locals in the crusty loaf of bread.

DESSERT

Amarula Don Pedro

Boozy cocktail made of Amarula Liqueur, ice cream and whipped cream. Perfect dessert for adults!

DRINK

South African Wine

South Africa is well known for its production of beautiful wines. Mainly, Jerepigo - sweet dessert wine and famous Pinotage, which is another local speciality not to be missed.
