Mexico

Mexico is a country known for chili-spiced cuisine, teeming cities and palm-fringed beaches.

MAIN DISHES

Chilaquiles

This popular traditional breakfast dish features lightly fried corn tortillas cut into quarters and topped with green or red salsa (the red is slightly spicier). Scrambled or fried eggs and pulled chicken are usually added on top, as well as cheese and cream. Chilaquiles are often served with a healthy dose of frijoles (refried beans).

Udon

Literally meaning toasted, tostadas are a simple but delicious dish involving corn tortillas fried in boiling oil until they become crunchy and golden. These are then served alone or piled high with any number of garnishes.

DESSERT

Sopapillas

These simple sopapillas are baked instead of fried and stuffed with a sweetened cream cheese filling.

DRINK

Cafe de Olla

Literally translating to coffee from a saucepan, it is a fairly watery, cinnamon flavoured coffee that is sweetened with piloncillo, unrefined cane sugar.
