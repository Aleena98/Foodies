GHANA

Ghananian people love to take advantage of the local crops and food source available in the best possible way creating beautiful dishes everyone should try! Did somebody say plaintain?

MAIN DISHES

Fried rice with chicken

Known as Jollof Rice - this is a traditional Ghananian dish created by combining freshest ingredients with beautiful spices and cripsy fried chicken.

Plantain

This is not to be missed! Best and the simplest food in Ghana, which can be eaten boiled, fried or cooked.

DESSERT

Strawberry fool

Combination of cream, wine and strawberry - perfect crowning point after hearty Ghananian main dishes.

DRINK

Sobolo

Tasty drink made of hibiscus leaves, infussed with ginger and dash of pineapple juice it has a very unique and sharp taste. Served chilled it goes best with spicy meals.

DID YOU KNOW?

Ghana is a home to the biggest open air market in West Africa. It is called Kejetia Market and locals and travellers shop here for traditional fabrics, footwear and garmets. It also has many food stalls with fresh fruit and meat.
