Thailand

Thailand is a southeast Asian country known for tropical beaches, royal palaces, ancient ruins and ornate temples.

MAIN DISHES

Phat Thai

Invented in 1930s, this is a popular dish of thin rice noodle stir-fried with egg, tofu and shrimp.

Phat Kaprao

This street food staple combines meat flash fried with holy basil and a generous helping of fresh chilli and garlic.

DESSERT

Mango sticky rice

Chilled mangos are sliced fresh before being put over steaming sticky rice and then drenched in thick coconut milk.

DRINK

Oliang - Thai iced coffee

Oliang is a mix of coffee and other ingredients, which depends on the recipe but can be roasted rice and caramel then roasted with coffee beans. This results in a unique flavor with a taste similar to Americano.
