MAIN DISHES

Pasta Al Forno

Direct Italian translation of Pasta Al Forno is "Pasta from the oven". Originally baked in the wood burning ovens. This tradition lives till now, as locals claim it tastes much better that way!

Risotto

This dish comes from Northern Italy. It is cooked with the broth until it becomes creamy. It is delicious, but when cooked at home requires a bit of patience, as it does take some time to make it perfect.

Tiramisu
