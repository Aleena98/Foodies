POLAND

Very hearty cuisine with butter and cream added to almost everything! Who wouldn't like that ? Big portions and many dishes with forest mushrooms and groats.

MAIN DISHES

Pierogi

If you have tried anything Polish then it was probably pierogi. Famous dumplings can be eaten as a main dish (meat or white cheese & potato filling) or as a dessert (blueberry filling).

Barszcz

Beetroot soup, which is traditionally served during Polish Christmas Eve.

DESSERT

Cheesecake

One of the most popular desserts in Poland. Sweet pastry bottom with a filling made of dry-cured cheese called "twarog". Very often has raisins inside, especially in the Christmas edition.

DRINK

Kompot

A bit of a surprise, but one of the most famous Polish drinks is a non-alocholic Kompot. Made of real fresh fruit, usually apples, strawberries or mixed forest berries, served cold with a lunch.

DID YOU KNOW?

The biggest meal in all Polish homes is lunch, eaten usually around 3PM. It consists of two dishes: first - soup, second - main dish (like pierogi).
