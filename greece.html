GREECE

Greek cuisine has a tradition of more than 4000 years and it plays a big role in culture and a history of Greece. With common use of fresh vegetables and fish it does not only taste amazing, but is also very healthy!

MAIN DISHES

Greek salad( Horiatiki)

Very basic, but deserves a mention. Cruchy cucumber, tomato & green pepper with black olives, authentic feta cheese and onion, all drizzled with olive oil. Simple, yet beautiful.

Dolma

Grape vine leaves stuffed with rice and herbs. Traditional and delicious dish served as an appetizer.

DESSERT

Halva

Very popular Greek dessert made with a mix of honey and sesame seeds. It is a particularly sweet dessert, which comes is different tastes like vanilla, pistacho or cocoa.

DRINK

Ouzo

It is considered the national drink of Greexe. Extremely strong (96%!) with added anise it goes great with strong-flavoured seafood and pickled peppers.

DID YOU KNOW?

Greeks use olive oil in almost every dish! They cook their meat with it, dip their bread and pour it over cheese. Tasty and healthy!
