MAIN DISHES

Rice Stew

One thing is certain - Nigerians love rice! That is why one of their most popular dish is a variations of rice and and vegetable stew with spices like ginger and curry.

Coconut fried rice

Simple yet delicious dish of creamy riced cooked in coconut milk along with added vegetables is match made in heaven.

DESSERT

Banana Ice Cream

Nigerians are not big on desserts, but one thing they have nailed - banana ice cream! Delicious and creamy!

DRINK

Kunu

Popular drink, especially in North Nigeria. Made from whole grains of millet or corn. It is super nutritious and rich in fiber and protein, also packed with minerals and lots of vitamins! Way to go Nigeria!
