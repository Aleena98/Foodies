Japan

Japan is a culturally diverse country, home to 21 World Heritage sites including Himeji Castle and Historic Monuments of Ancient Tokyo.

MAIN DISHES

Sushi

It is made with vinegared rice and fresh fish and is presented in a various ways and shapes.

Udon

A dense and chewy noodle made from wheat flour, Udon is widely known for its versatility and delicious taste.

DESSERT

Dorayaki

Chilled mangos are sliced fresh before being put over steaming sticky rice and then drenched in thick coconut milk.

DRINK

Melon Soda

Melon soda is one of the most popular soft drinks in Japan. Melon soda has a bright green color and goes great together with ice cream in melon soda floats.

DID YOU KNOW?

While the popularity of rice may be somewhat declining in Japan, it is still very common to eat rice for breakfast, lunch and dinner. Historically, rice has always played an important role in Japanese cuisine.
